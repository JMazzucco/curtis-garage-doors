BRIEF DESCRIPTION

This elite series belt drive opener from LiftMaster is equipped with a battery backup, which means you can get in and out of your garage every time - even during a power outage.

FEATURES

Model 8550

  • MyQ® technology enables you to securely monitor and control your garage door opener with your smartphone, tablet or computer.
  • Energy-efficient operation consumes up to 75% less power in standby mode.
  • Battery backup powers up even when the power's out
  • 1/2 Horsepower motor, equipped with Motor Vibration Isolation System.
  • Belt drive
  • Timer-to-close remembers to close your door after a set time if you forget to.
  • One piece-solid steel-T-Rail with automatic belt tensioner on trolley.
  • Can suit up to 10’ high openings
  • Smart control panel & three button elite remote control
  • Lifetime Warranty on the motor and the belt
