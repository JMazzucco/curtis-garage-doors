BRIEF DESCRIPTION

This heavy duty Elite Series Opener is equipped with 3/4 HP motor, making it an ideal choice for heavier garage doors.

FEATURES

Model 8557

  • 3/4 HP motor combines a strong pulling force with a steel reinforced belt to lift heavy doors and reinforced doors in high wind areas.
  • MyQ® technology enables you to securely monitor and control your garage door opener with your smartphone, tablet or computer.
  • Energy-efficient operation consumes up to 75% less power in standby mode.
  • Equipped with Motor Vibration Isolation System
  • Can suit up to 10’ high openings
  • Timer-to-close remembers to close your door after a set time if you forget to.
  • Accessories included: Smart control panel (easy to program and maintance alerts on LCD screen) & three button elite remote control.
  • Lifetime Warranty on the motor and the belt
