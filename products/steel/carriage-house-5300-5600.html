BRIEF DESCRIPTION

The 5300/5600 Series Steel Carriage House Door is truly an innovative product. It has been designed to give you, the homeowner, an historically accurate re-creation of the traditional wood carriage house door. This model provides the maintenance-free durability of steel and a limited lifetime warranty for years of worry-free protection.

A durable, maintenance-free steel exterior, overlaid with poly accent battens creates the timeless look of the 5300 and 5600 series from CHI Overhead Doors. A strong steel base section provides the foundation for an addition to your home that will last for years to come.
