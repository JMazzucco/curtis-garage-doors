BRIEF DESCRIPTION

These strong, traditional stylish steel doors are built to safeguard against the elements and be structurally rigid. Durable as well as attractive, these doors come in seven beautiful colors.

FEATURES

  • Model 2216/4216
  • Model 2283/4283
  • Model 2240/4240

Model 2216 (short panel)/4216 (long panel)

  • Door Exterior sections are formed from 26-gauge galvanized draw quality steel and the interior is 27-gauge steel.
  • High-Density CFC free polyurethane injected core
  • Concealed steel plates run continuously from top to bottom of each section for fastening hardware.
  • R-value 17.19

Model 2283 (short panel)/4283 (long panel)

  • Door sections are formed from 27-gauge galvanized draw quality steel.
  • With CFC free polystrene insulation core
  • Section joints are roll-formed into a tongue-and-groove design which produces a superior weather seal.
  • Concealed steel plates run continuously from top to bottom of each section for fastening hardware.
  • Double sided steel doors are 2" thick.
  • R-value 9.65

Model 2240 (short panel)/4240 (long panel)

  • Door sections are formed from 24-gauge galvanized draw quality steel.
  • Section joints are roll-formed into a tongue-and-groove design which produces a superior weather seal.
  • Inside stiles are made of galvanized steel and stile holes are "dimpled" for increased holding power.
  • Non-insulated model

Available Colours

  • White
  • Almond
  • Sandstone
  • Brown
  • Evergreen
  • Silver (only available in 2283/4283)
  • Bronze (not available in 2240/4240)
