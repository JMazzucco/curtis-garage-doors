BRIEF DESCRIPTION

The 5200/5900 Series Steel Carriage House Door is truly an innovative product. It has been designed to give you, the homeowner, an historically accurate re-creation of the traditional wood carriage house door. This model provides the maintenance-free durability of steel and a limited lifetime warranty for years of worry-free protection.

With a diverse collection of window, hardware and color options, you can be certain to find the door that will complement your home perfectly.

New to this popular line is the Accent Series Collection. Accent wood tones which create the allure of solid wood doors are available in cedar, mahogany, dark oak and light oak.

Model 5216/5916

The 5216 (short panel) and 5916 (long panel) are fabricated with a foam injection process using CFC free polyurethane foam completely filling the core of this product. PVC extrusions at both section joints add the protection of a continuous weather seal. The combination of this high degree of structural integrity with an exceptional R-value rating makes this model the first in its class.

Model 5283/5983

The 5283 (short panel) and 5983 (long panel) are double-sided steel doors with CFC free polystyrene insulation. This will offer protection against the natural elements while adding structural integrity to the door. The 5283 or 5983 will provide direct architectural appeal and add value to your home for years to come.

Model 5250

Beautify your home with the durable, single layer construction of our 5240 model. With an appealing design and a sturdy 24-gauge steel construction, the 5400 model makes this door an excellent choice. Available in short panel only.
