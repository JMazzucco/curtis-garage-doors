BRIEF DESCRIPTION

The Therma Classic Collection is our most versatile series of Carriage House doors. This recessed panel door features aluminum overlays to create a wide variety of designs.

Preserve the traditional character of your home without the maintenance hassle of wood doors. Keep the crisp white finish, or easily paint them to match your exact colour requirements.
