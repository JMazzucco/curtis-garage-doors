BRIEF DESCRIPTION

Adored for their likeness to wood doors, these Recessed Panel polyurethane insulated steel doors are maintenance free! The exterior wood grain embossed steel as well as the overlays are all metal and the sections are joined with a tongue and groove design that add up to optimum weather protection. A great alternative to our wood designs.
