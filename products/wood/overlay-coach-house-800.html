DESCRIPTION

With today’s increase in custom home building and renovations, homeowners are choosing unique looks that will set their home apart from others. The Overlay Coach House Series Doors are hand crafted by local carpenters, using only the finest woods to finish off the perfect picture of class and quality and adding a natural tone to the look of your home.

The 800 Series features Mahogany wood moulding joined seamlessly over the marine-grade Mahogany door panels. The final product resembles a beautiful handcrafted picture frame.

FEATURES

  • Constructed of Flush Wood Insulated sections with Marine-grade Plywood on the front and Mahogany Plywood on the back.
  • Mahogany Moulding Overlay designs as per model chart. Custom designs available.
  • All models are handcrafted in-house.
  • Custom arch designs are available to suit your garage door frames.
  • 1/2” Thermal glass options are available: Clear, Opaque, Tinted.
  • Great for Staining!
