BRIEF DESCRIPTION

There's nothing like the look of a PolyPro garage door. They are preferred by many for their likeness to wood, affordability and durability. The panels are made from recycled plastics and joined together with solid hemlock wood rails.

FEATURES

Polypro doors are available in short panel or long panel design with a variety of window styles to chose from.
